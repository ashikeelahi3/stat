# Product Overview

## Project Purpose and Value Proposition

**STAT** is a comprehensive survey research platform designed for academic studies, specifically focused on psychological and behavioral research. The platform enables researchers to conduct sophisticated experimental designs with built-in data quality controls and randomization features.

### Core Mission
- Facilitate rigorous academic research through digital survey tools
- Provide robust data collection with quality assurance mechanisms
- Enable complex experimental designs with randomized controlled trials
- Support psychological research methodologies with statistical rigor

## Key Features and Capabilities

### Survey Design and Management
- **Multi-part Survey Structure**: Supports complex survey designs with multiple sections (financial stress assessment, career confidence measurement, control variables)
- **Question Randomization**: Built-in randomization for question order to test priming effects
- **Attention Check Integration**: Automated attention check questions to ensure data quality
- **Response Validation**: Real-time validation and required field enforcement

### Experimental Research Tools
- **A/B Testing Framework**: Randomized assignment to different survey versions
- **Order Effect Studies**: Capability to test question-order effects on responses
- **Priming Mechanisms**: Tools to implement psychological priming in survey flow
- **Control Variable Collection**: Systematic collection of demographic and control variables

### Data Quality Assurance
- **Attention Check System**: Multiple types of attention checks with randomized placement
- **Response Time Tracking**: Detailed timing data for each question and overall survey
- **Speeder Detection**: Automatic flagging of responses completed too quickly
- **Straight-lining Detection**: Identification of uniform response patterns

### User Experience Features
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly interface with dark/light mode support
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress indicators throughout survey completion
- **Gamification Elements**: Integrated reward system (Coin Catch game) to improve engagement
- **Anonymous Participation**: Privacy-focused design encouraging honest responses

### Analytics and Reporting
- **Real-time Data Collection**: Immediate data capture with timestamp tracking
- **Export Capabilities**: SPSS, R, and Python-ready data formats
- **Statistical Analysis Support**: Pre-configured variables for common statistical tests
- **Quality Metrics Dashboard**: Response quality indicators and exclusion criteria

## Target Users and Use Cases

### Primary Users
- **Academic Researchers**: Psychology, sociology, and behavioral science researchers
- **Graduate Students**: Thesis and dissertation research projects
- **Research Institutions**: Universities and research centers conducting survey studies
- **Statistical Analysts**: Data scientists working with experimental survey data

### Use Cases

#### Academic Research
- **Question-Order Effect Studies**: Testing how question sequence influences responses
- **Priming Research**: Investigating psychological priming effects in survey contexts
- **Behavioral Economics**: Studies on financial stress and decision-making
- **Career Development Research**: Assessment of career confidence and future planning

#### Data Collection Scenarios
- **Cross-sectional Studies**: Single-point-in-time data collection
- **Experimental Designs**: Randomized controlled trials with survey instruments
- **Longitudinal Preparation**: Foundation for follow-up studies
- **Pilot Testing**: Small-scale validation of survey instruments

#### Quality Control Applications
- **Response Validation**: Ensuring participant attention and engagement
- **Data Cleaning Automation**: Systematic exclusion of low-quality responses
- **Methodological Rigor**: Meeting publication standards for academic journals
- **Replication Studies**: Standardized protocols for research reproducibility

### Value Delivered
- **Research Efficiency**: Streamlined data collection with automated quality controls
- **Methodological Rigor**: Built-in experimental design best practices
- **Data Integrity**: Comprehensive quality assurance mechanisms
- **Publication Readiness**: Analysis-ready datasets with proper documentation
- **Cost Effectiveness**: Reduced need for manual data cleaning and validation
- **Scalability**: Support for studies ranging from pilot tests to large-scale research
